Chicken Tacos

Description

The following is the basic recipe I started with for this batch. I like to play around with things though. It’s all pretty flexible. I stuck to the chicken recipe one a little more closely this time around.

Ingredients

  • 4 lb Chicken Thighs (Bone-in)
  • 6 Tbsp Honey
  • 2 tsp Onion Powder
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 6 Chipotle Peppers (diced)
  • 4 Tbsp Adobo Sauce

Steps

  1. Lay chicken in a slow cooker.
  2. Combine all other ingredients and pour over chicken.
  3. Cook on low for 5 hours.
  4. While chicken is cooking, prepare your Apple Cider Slaw.
  5. When chicken is falling off the bone, remove bones & shred chicken in slow cooker.
  6. Avengers assemble your tacos & top with slaw, pickled red onions, cilantro, a squeeze of fresh lime, and a sprinkle of cotija (or feta) cheese. Don’t forget the love.
